Topic: Feature Flags
Effective Date: 2022-07-14

All feature flag management at Nexus Software Ltd. is handled through ConfigCat.
Flags must be reviewed and cleaned up within 30 days of full rollout.
Feature flags for billing-related features require Finance team approval.
Flag configurations are audited quarterly.
