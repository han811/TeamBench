Topic: Sla Targets
Effective Date: 2024-01-19

Current Value: 99.95%

The Nexus Software Ltd. production environment is committed to an uptime SLA of 99.95%.
SLA breaches must be disclosed in the monthly service report.
Planned maintenance windows do not count against SLA measurement.
Customer credits for SLA breaches are defined in the customer agreement.
