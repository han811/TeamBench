POLICY / SPECIFICATION

Topic: Pricing Model
Effective Date: 2021-03-28

Current Value: usage-based billing

Nexus Software Ltd. standard tier products are sold using usage-based billing.
Pricing is reviewed annually by the Finance and Product committees.
Custom pricing arrangements require VP-level approval.
All pricing changes must be communicated to customers 30 days in advance.
